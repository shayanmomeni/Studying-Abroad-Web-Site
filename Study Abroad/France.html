Has France always been your dream study destination?

Well, you have come to the right place. France especially Paris is One of the top destinations for international students. The universities in France rank among the best in the world. The cost of living in France is relatively cheaper than the equally popular countries.

At the beginning of every term, each campus conducts an orientation program especially designed to assist incoming students in adjusting to a new country. Lectures are planned to introduce students not only to some of the customs and cultural heritage of the host country, but to the practical aspects of living in that country as well.

Students receive information about public transportation schedules and fares, banking procedures and hours, student discounts, inexpensive restaurants, and local customs. Every effort is made to ease the transition to life in a new country and to answer all questions students may have about the school and the host country. We also help you to deal with things like visa and residence permits, driver licenses, etc.

All our programs are taught in English to make sure our students gain a major competitive advantage, by mastering the language of business “par excellence”, by the time they finish their studies. What is Paris life cost as students?

You just got an opportunity do make a mobility exchange in Paris!! Keep calm and take it easy, we've listed you the main cost that you will have by living in this wonderful and charming city of love call Paris. Before you start to read this, we want to let you know that all the information here is given as indicative resource. Your own life cost experience might be different!

Rent - Accommodation:

Housing will be your main expenses. Students' flat between 9m² to 20m² will cost you approximately between 450€ to 700€ per month. To find cheap housing in Paris we advise you to have a look on our accommodation section. You can lower the rent by living in Hall of Residence, living in a double room or sharing a flat with flat mates.

To keep it simple someone who live in Paris will pay around 70€ per month.

Required application documents for France study visa

1. Proof of payment of the visa fee (99 EUR).
2. Visa application form
3. Two passport type photos
4. Valid passport and copies of your previous visas
5. Official enrollment or acceptance letter from the university
6. IELTS result (your counsellor can book your place in an English test)
7. Academic and work experience documents
8. Statement of purpose
9. Proof of financial means (around 615 EUR per month) to cover all your expenses during your study period
10. Airline ticket/reservation showing date of departure
11.Medical insurance that might cost between 311 and 714 EUR/year
12.Documents stating the accommodation arrangement during your studies
13. Proof of civil status (if applicable)
